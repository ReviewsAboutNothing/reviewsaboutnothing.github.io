17/11/2024 - us. (feat. Taylor Swift) - Gracie Abrams, Taylor Swift - Music Reviews About Nothing

Short Review:

Rating: 10/10

'us. (feat. Taylor Swift) is a song that develops from a simple accoustic into a passionate clash of emotions.

Its lyrics start with a reflection on a past relationship and end with a mighty bridge.

Vinyl Mockup:

The cover art is a from when Taylor Swift brought on Gracie Abrams for her London Eras Tour show to sing 'us. (feat. Taylor Swift)' together.

The vinyl is the colour of Gracie's 'The Secret of Us' album, and has a slight marbling in different, darker yellows.
