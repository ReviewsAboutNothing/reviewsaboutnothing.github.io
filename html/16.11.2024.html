16/11/2024 - Free Now - Gracie Abrams - Music Reviews About Nothing

Short Review:

Rating: 10/10

'Free Now' by Gracie Abrams is a unique song, with a powerful bridge and lack of a repeating chorus.

Its lyrics show how she looks at a break-up in a neutral way, seeing the positives without any anger.

Vinyl Mockup:

The cover art is a still from Gracie's music video for 'Risk'. It also shows the name of the song in a yellow italic font, found on the promotional text for 'The Secret of Us'.

The sole yellow of the vinyl is found on the album, and matches the text.
