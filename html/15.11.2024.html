15/11/2024 - Hello goodbye - Charli xcx Music Reviews About Nothing

Short Review:

Rating: 6/10

'Hello goodbye' by Charli xcx is a nostalgic, toned-down and vulnerable track, especially when compared to the rest of her album BRAT.

Its lyrics show regret at how she introduced herself to a potential future partner, in a quite sensitive way.

Vinyl Mockup:

The cover art is a still from Charli's music video for her song 'Von dutch'. It has a neon blue band on the left hand side, similar to her other singles from her BRAT era.

'Hello goodbye' is a bonus track from the deluxe version of BRAT. The album cover for that album is black and white, which is reflected in the splatter of the mockup.
